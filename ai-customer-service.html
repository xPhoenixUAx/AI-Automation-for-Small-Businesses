Example workflow

From incoming message to owned next step

A safe workflow keeps the original request, explains what the AI did, and makes ownership visible.

  • Capture messages from approved channels
  • Detect topic, urgency, and missing context
  • Retrieve only relevant approved information
  • Draft or route according to defined risk rules
  • Record the action and notify an owner

Guardrails

Helpful replies need reliable boundaries

An assistant should know what information it may use, what it may draft, what it must never promise, and when a person must step in.

Monitoring should cover incorrect routing, unsupported answers, repeated edits, customer dissatisfaction, and any case where the workflow could not find enough context.

  • Refunds, discounts, and financial commitments
  • Complaints, disputes, or emotionally sensitive situations
  • Requests involving private or regulated information
  • Missing or contradictory approved information
