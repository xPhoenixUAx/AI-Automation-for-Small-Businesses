Brand and consent

Speed should not remove accountability

AI-generated marketing content can be inaccurate, off-brand, or inappropriate if it is published without review.

We design human approval into consequential steps, restrict inputs to approved information, and define when the automation must stop for a decision.

  • Approved source and brand guidance
  • Named reviewer and publishing permission
  • Consent, unsubscribe, and frequency rules
  • Fallback when data or context is incomplete
