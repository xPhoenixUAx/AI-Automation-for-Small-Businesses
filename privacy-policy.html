Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information this website receives, why it is used, and the choices available to you.

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Scope and contact

This policy applies to the FlowReady AI website and the contact and automation-audit forms available on it. FlowReady AI acts as the party responsible for the personal information submitted through this website.

Privacy questions and requests can be sent to hello@flowready.example.

Information we receive

When you submit a form or email us, we may receive your name, email address, company, inquiry type, budget range, preferred timeline, current tools, team size, message, workflow description, source page, and any other information you choose to provide.

The hosting environment may also create ordinary server logs such as IP address, date and time, requested page, browser or device information, referral information, and technical error details. This website uses essential local browser storage to remember that the cookie notice was dismissed.

Please do not submit passwords, credentials, payment-card data, health information, government identifiers, or other sensitive personal information through the general forms.

Where information comes from

Most personal information is collected directly from you when you complete a form, send an email, request an audit, or continue a business conversation. We may also receive ordinary business contact information from a colleague who introduces you, from a company website or professional directory, or from a service provider that helps deliver a message to us.

If another person provides information about you, they should have authority to do so and should direct you to this policy where appropriate. We do not intentionally purchase consumer profiles or combine inquiry information with unrelated advertising data.

Why we use information

  • To receive, review, and respond to your inquiry.
  • To evaluate a requested audit, service, advertising opportunity, collaboration, or partnership.
  • To maintain the security, availability, and integrity of the website and forms.
  • To prevent spam, abuse, and repeated automated submissions.
  • To keep business records and comply with applicable legal obligations.

Depending on the circumstances and applicable law, processing may be based on taking steps at your request, our legitimate interests in responding and operating a secure website, your consent where requested, or a legal obligation.

Business communication and marketing

We may follow up about the specific request you submitted and closely related services. We do not intend to add a general inquiry to an unrelated promotional mailing list without an appropriate basis. You can ask us to stop non-essential marketing messages at any time; service-related or legally required communications may still be sent where necessary.

AI and automated processing

Information submitted through an inquiry may be organized, categorized, summarized, or routed with the help of automation or AI tools. These tools are intended to assist routine administration and do not replace appropriate human review of proposals, scope, pricing, or sensitive decisions.

The website does not use submitted inquiry information to make a solely automated decision that produces legal or similarly significant effects for you. If a future service changes that practice, the relevant notice and safeguards will be provided before the change applies.

Sharing and service providers

We do not sell personal information. Information may be handled by providers needed to operate the website and email, such as a hosting provider, email service, security provider, or professional adviser. They should receive only the information needed for their role and be subject to appropriate contractual or legal duties.

Information may also be disclosed when required by law, to protect rights or safety, to investigate abuse, or as part of a business reorganization with appropriate safeguards.

International data transfers

Website, email, security, or business-service providers may operate in countries other than the country where you are located. When applicable law requires safeguards for an international transfer, we will seek to use an approved transfer mechanism, contractual protection, or another lawful basis and will evaluate supplementary measures where appropriate.

No internet-based service can guarantee that information will remain in one country. You may contact us for more information about the safeguards relevant to your inquiry.

Retention

Inquiry information is kept only as long as reasonably needed to respond, evaluate a potential engagement, maintain appropriate business records, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations. The retention period depends on whether a conversation becomes a project, whether follow-up is reasonably expected, and whether tax, accounting, contract, security, or dispute records must be preserved.

Server-log retention is determined by the hosting provider and security needs. Backups may retain residual copies for a limited period before they are overwritten. Information that is no longer needed should be deleted, de-identified, or anonymized where practical.

Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect information. Measures may include access controls, provider review, least-privilege practices, software maintenance, backups, and procedures for responding to suspected incidents. However, no website, email, or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure. You are responsible for choosing an appropriate channel and limiting the information you submit.

Data incidents

If we become aware of a personal-data incident, we will investigate, take reasonable containment and remediation steps, and notify affected individuals or authorities when applicable law requires it. A notification may be delayed when law enforcement or another legal restriction requires delay.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live and which law applies, you may have the right to:

  • Ask whether we process personal information about you and request access to it.
  • Correct information that is inaccurate or complete information that is incomplete.
  • Request deletion or restriction in circumstances provided by law.
  • Object to certain processing, including direct marketing.
  • Receive eligible information in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for future processing where consent is the basis used.
  • Request human involvement where applicable automated-decision rights exist.
  • Complain to the privacy or data-protection authority responsible for your location.

These rights are not absolute. A request may be limited where information must be preserved to comply with law, protect another person, maintain security, establish or defend legal claims, or satisfy another permitted exception.

How to make a request

Email us using the address above, identify the right you want to exercise, and provide enough context to locate the relevant information. We may ask for reasonable verification and, where permitted, clarification of a broad request. Authorized agents should provide evidence of their authority. We will respond within the period required by applicable law and explain any lawful extension or refusal.

We will not discriminate against you for exercising an applicable privacy right. You may also contact the relevant supervisory authority directly if you believe your information has been handled unlawfully.

Business customer and service data

If FlowReady AI later processes personal information on behalf of a customer while delivering an automation service, the customer may be the controller or business responsible for that information and FlowReady AI may act as its processor or service provider. In that situation, the project agreement and any data-processing terms govern the service data. Requests about data controlled by that customer should normally be directed to the customer first.

Children

This business website is not directed to children, and the forms are not intended for information from anyone under the age at which they can provide valid consent in their jurisdiction. If you believe a child submitted information, contact us so it can be reviewed and removed where appropriate.

External links and third-party websites

The website may link to third-party services or resources. Their privacy practices are controlled by their own policies, not this one. Following a link, using an integration, or interacting with an embedded service may allow that provider to collect information under its own terms.

Browser privacy signals

Because this website does not currently use behavioral advertising or cross-site analytics, it does not separately respond to “Do Not Track” signals. If technology covered by a legally recognized opt-out preference signal is introduced, we will evaluate and honor the signal where required.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the website, services, providers, or legal requirements change. The date at the top will be revised when changes are published. If a change materially affects how previously collected information is used, we will provide additional notice where reasonably possible or legally required.
