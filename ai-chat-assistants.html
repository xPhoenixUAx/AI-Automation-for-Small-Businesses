AI Chat Assistants

Give customers or staff a faster way to find approved information and complete routine tasks—with clear boundaries and an obvious route to a person.

Discuss an assistant
Start with one job

A useful assistant has a defined purpose

“Answer anything” is not a reliable requirement. The assistant needs an audience, approved knowledge, allowed actions, and escalation rules.

It might help website visitors understand services, help staff find an internal procedure, or collect the right information before handing a request to the team.

  • Answer only from approved, maintained sources
  • State uncertainty instead of inventing an answer
  • Collect the minimum information needed
  • Hand sensitive or unusual questions to a person
A FOCUSED ASSISTANT

Clear scope in, useful support out

  • Who will use it?
  • What questions may it answer?
  • Which documents are authoritative?
  • What information may it collect?
  • When must it escalate?
  • Who updates and reviews the knowledge?
Assistant patterns

Choose the smallest useful role

Different audiences and risks call for different knowledge, permissions, wording, and handoff design.

Website service guide

Explain approved services, service areas, prerequisites, and next steps before routing the visitor to a form or team member.

Internal knowledge helper

Help staff locate policies, checklists, templates, and process notes from an approved knowledge base.

Inquiry intake assistant

Ask relevant questions, structure the answers, and create a complete handoff without making commitments on the team’s behalf.

Routine task copilot

Draft summaries, prepare checklists, or transform information while requiring review before consequential actions.

Customer support triage

Identify the topic and urgency, suggest an approved next step, and route the conversation with its context intact.

Document navigator

Find relevant passages across controlled documents and show the source so the user can verify the response.

Response path

Every answer follows a controlled route

01UnderstandIdentify the question and required context.
02RetrieveFind relevant approved information.
03RespondGive a bounded answer with useful next steps.
04EscalateTransfer uncertainty, risk, or exceptions.
Ongoing governance

The knowledge needs an owner

An assistant can only remain trustworthy if its information, conversations, permissions, and failure patterns are reviewed.

We define update responsibility, test questions, source visibility, data-retention choices, and a regular review process before the assistant becomes part of daily work.

  • Known sourcesOnly selected documents or systems.
  • Human routeA person is always reachable.
  • Safe data useCollection stays purpose-limited.
  • Review loopWeak answers become improvements.
